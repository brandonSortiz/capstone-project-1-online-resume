Brandon's Hobbies

Here is a small list of my favorite hobbies to do on my free time!

  1. Kendama - This is a japanese skill toy that not only is a fun way to pass the time but build your hand eye coordination! There are an infinite amount of tricks that you can learn and attempt to create a line which consists of back to back tricks!

  2. Spikeball - This is a new sport that utilizes a small circular net/trampoline placed on the floor, played with teams of 2. A player starts a point by serving the small bouncy ball onto the Spikeball net so it ricochets up towards his opponent.

  3. Videogames - I have been playing videos games ever since I was about 10 years old when my father got me a gameboy advanced with the game pokemon fire red! As of recent I have been playing the soul games created by FromSoftware, Inc.

  4. Getting Jacked - The summer of 2022 I decided I wanted to make a change and build myself a bigger frame. I started going to the gym 5-7 days a week consistently and increasing my protein and calorie intake. I went from weighing 135 pounds to a killer 190 pounds in a matter of a year and a half!
